XLab Mobile

XLab Mobile is a UC Berkeley endeavor to take social science out of the white room. Common criticisms of social science experiments are that a lab, typically a room on campus with some desktops, is a contrived environment, and that subjects, typically college undergraduates, are from a narrow demographic. By implementing experiments on mobile phones, Berkeley researchers seek to address both of these criticisms.
